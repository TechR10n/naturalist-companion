# Use Case - Naturalist Flaneur

## Scenario

A user walks through a park or neighborhood, taking occasional photos and asking the app to suggest what to notice next.

## Goals

- Encourage observation and curiosity.
- Provide short, grounded explanations with citations.
- Offer next-step prompts ("photograph leaf underside", "capture bark texture").

## Flow

1. User opens the app and selects "Explore".
2. The app suggests 2-3 nearby questions.
3. The user captures a photo or asks a question.
4. The app returns structured hypotheses, features, and citations.

## Success criteria

- Users can complete a session in 3-5 minutes.
- Follow-up prompts lead to a better second result.
- The experience feels guided, not overwhelming.
